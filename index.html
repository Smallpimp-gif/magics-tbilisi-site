Brief

1

Drawings & spec

Send a DXF/PDF drawing or just describe the part. We confirm dimensions, plywood grade (laminated or raw), tolerances and quantities — usually within the same day.

Cutting

2

CNC milling

Five-axis Italian CNC beds at ±0.4 mm tolerance. Reels, trolleys, jigs, custom parts — anything that fits a 3,600 mm bed runs in a single setup.

Finish

3

Sanding

Three grits, hand-finished where it shows, machine-sanded where it doesn't. Edges eased so cable doesn't catch on dispatch.

QC

4

Quality check.

Every batch goes through a 22-point checklist — dimensions, moisture, lamination integrity, load tolerance. Failures are scrapped, not patched. Photo report on request.

Dispatch

5

Pack & ship

Heat-treated EUR pallets, banded and QR-tagged. Incoterms EXW Batumi, FCA Poti, or DAP to your warehouse — your call.

Frequently asked

If your question isn't here — message WhatsApp and we'll reply within an hour during business hours (UTC+4).

  • What's the minimum order?+
    From 20 units of one model. Smaller batches available from finished-goods stock if your size is in. Custom builds from 50 units.
  • What are the lead times?+
    Standard sizes from the table — 7–14 working days. Custom drawings — up to four weeks. Exact lead time depends on current floor load and is confirmed in your quote.
  • What are the payment terms?+
    Default: 50% deposit, 50% before dispatch. Net-30 by agreement for repeat customers. We accept USD, EUR, GBP and GEL by bank transfer.
  • Do you make custom reels?+
    Yes. We accept DXF / DWG / PDF. If you only have parameters (cable weight, OD, winding length), we'll size the geometry for you. Drawings are signed off before tooling.
  • How does shipping to the EU work?+
    Sea freight from Poti or Batumi in 20'/40' containers — typical transit 10–18 days to North-EU ports. CE certificate of compliance and packing list included. Incoterms EXW Batumi, FCA Poti, or DAP to your warehouse by agreement.
  • Can I get a sample?+
    Small-diameter samples (Ø600/800) shipped free against a confirmed batch enquiry. Express courier at customer's expense.
  • What certifications do you provide?+
    Quality passport with measurements per batch. Company-wide: FSC®, ISO 9001:2015, EN 13388 / CE, REACH compliance. Full PDF copies sent with tender packs.
  • What's included in the price?+
    Finished reel to drawing, with hardware, sanded, QR-tagged, on heat-treated EUR pallet, banded. Not included: freight (line-itemed separately), customs clearance at destination.
